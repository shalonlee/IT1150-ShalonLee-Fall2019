Pan's Labyrinth

This movie is about a little girl named Ofelia who creates her own fantasy world during a war in Spain. Ofelia's mother marries a cruel Captain that leads Ofelia to escape into her own fantasy known as Pan's Labyrinth. Pan's Labyrinth is a wonderfully dark fantasy spoken in Spanish directed by Guillermo del Toro. For more information, please check out IMBD.
